DC Emergency Rental Assistance Amendment Act — What It Means for You
The DC Council passed Amendment 2024-B extending emergency rental assistance through December 2025. Eligible renters can apply for up to $3,000 in back-rent relief if they experienced income loss after Jan 2024.
✓
Renters earning under 80% AMI ($76,000 for a family of 3) qualify
✓
Applications open at dhs.dc.gov starting March 1
✓
Landlords cannot begin eviction proceedings during application review
Source: DC Register Vol. 71 No. 8
